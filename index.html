How Evento.lk works


Are you interested in becoming an Evento.lk professional and getting customers easily?
Great! Here's how the Evento.lk works:
Evento.lk provides online booking services. We act as an intermediary (agent) between customers wanting to book professionals and you. This type of business model is also known as an 'agency model'.
It's important to remember that the agreement is made directly between you and the customers. Unlike other agency websites, Evento.lk is not a contracting party in the transaction between you and the customers. That way you have the full control over your rates and availability, and can offer customers more transparency on your rules and overall offering. The customers pay the booking rate to you after your service or earlier in case of non-refundable booking. You have an agreement with Evento.lk for using the online event planning platform and you have to pay a commission amount for a selected period of time when getting registered.
We don't add anything to your prices. Unlike other platforms that charge customers extra fees, the price you set is the price we display on Evento.lk. You can also communicate the price rates personally with the customers. There are no other costs.
